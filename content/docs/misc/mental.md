Mental health is a severely underlooked aspect in this society. As someone who suffered from severe depression and suicidal thoughts, I would like to share my toolbox with anyone who sees this page.

# Cognitive Distortions
**Types**
- **All-or-Nothing thinking**: perfect success or disastrous failure.
- **Overgeneralizing**: one instance = all instances.
- **Black-n-White thinking**: everything is black or white.
- **Mindreading**: assume your assumption of other people's thoughts is always correct.
- **Catastrophizing**: when something uncertain takes place, automatically assume the worst case.
- **Emotional reasoning**: emotional thoughts = rational thoughts.
- **Labeling**: one mistake or failure labels the person's entire identity.
- **Mental filtering**: focus on the negatives, ignore the positives or neutrals.
- **Personalization**: everything is under your control, of your responsibility, and your fault.
- **Unreal ideal**: if one does not reach perfection, one does not deserve to live.

&nbsp;

**Why do they exist?**
- They have good intentions. They are meant to be beneficial to us via short-term avoidance of responsibilities, risks, and vulnerabilities. They are part of our self-defense mechanism so that we feel secure in this world full of danger.
- However, they have awful executions. They are not beneficial in the long term.

&nbsp;

**What to do? (CBT ver.)**
1. Notice and name it.
    - Check for extreme keywords, curse words, and assumptions.
2. Understand what benefit you get from it.
3. Challenge it.
    1. What would the most rational being say about it to you?
    2. What would you say about it if your best friend had it?
    3. Find counterexamples.
    4. See both sides of the coin.
    5. Remove extreme words and reframe the sentence.
    6. What happens if you just assume the opposite for a second?
    7. What happens if you just stop having this thought for a second?

&nbsp;

**What to do? (Cognitive Defusion ver.)**
- In the cases where emotions take the upper hand, ration simply does not work. You have to give up ration and think outside the box.
- Procedure:
    1.	Start by saying "I am having the thought that …"
    2.	Do it again by saying "**I notice** that I am having the thought that …"
    3.	Name your mind with an obviously stupid and negative character.
    4.	Say or sing the thoughts in their stupid voices.
    5.	Put the thoughts onto an object, then imagine burning it, throwing rocks at it, or whatever. Just destroy that cursed object.
    6.	Say "Thank you mind!"
